Frequently Asked Questions

What is ATB about?

Activities that make you aware of the workings of your inner self—emotions, memories, thoughts, programs—and create harmony in your inner and outer world.


What is ATB and Neuroplasticity?

Blowing away decades of scientific dogma, the recently discovered "neuroplastic" nature of the brain means that our potential is not set at birth — we can actually strengthen and improve our brain in ways once believed impossible. ATB is a Brain Workout.

Is ATB permanent?

ATB is not a technique. It is a whole language. Once you learn it, and use it and speak it, you can make it a part of yourself.
